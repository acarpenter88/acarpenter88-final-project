About Me

By definition a System Administrator is someone who is responsible for configuring and maintaining computer systems for a company. But what is it really to be a SysAdmin? It means wearing many hats, being able to fix email, configure servers, program switches, fix cell phones, and many other daily tasks the modern business needs to keep running smoothly.

I've been working in IT for 5 years. I started as an entry level tech in October of 2010, however, by October of 2011 I was the sole SysAdmin for a manufacturing plant in Oregon, OH within the same company. After 3 years of long hours in the Automotive Industry I went looking for a new challenge. In September of 2013 I joined a small consulting company and worked directly with customers from a variety of industries. Unfortunately the rigours of consulting were forcing me to put my ambition to continue higher education on the back burner. In September of 2014 I joined Midwest Tape, LLC, where I am currently employed as a Systems Administrator once again.
